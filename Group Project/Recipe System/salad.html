Fresh Garden Salad

Ingredients

  • 4 cups romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup radishes, sliced
  • 1/4 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and thoroughly dry all vegetables.
  2. Chop the romaine lettuce and add it to a large salad bowl.
  3. Add the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, carrots, and radishes to the bowl.
  4. In a small bowl or jar, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, and Dijon mustard until emulsified.
  5. Season the dressing with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat all the vegetables evenly.
  7. Top with croutons and Parmesan cheese if desired.
  8. Serve immediately for the freshest flavor and crunch.

Nutrition Information

Per serving (based on 4 servings):

  • Calories: 140
  • Protein: 4g
  • Carbohydrates: 12g
  • Fat: 9g
