Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups whole milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Maple syrup and butter for serving
  • Fresh berries (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined — do not overmix; lumps are okay.
  4. Let the batter rest for 5 minutes while you heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  5. Lightly grease the skillet with butter or cooking spray.
  6. Pour approximately 1/4 cup of batter per pancake onto the skillet.
  7. Cook until bubbles form on the surface and the edges look set, about 2–3 minutes.
  8. Flip and cook for another 1–2 minutes until golden brown.
  9. Serve warm with maple syrup, butter, and fresh berries if desired.

Nutrition Information

Per serving (based on 4 servings, ~3 pancakes each):

  • Calories: 320
  • Protein: 8g
  • Carbohydrates: 48g
  • Fat: 11g
