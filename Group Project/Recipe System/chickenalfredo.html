Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 2lbs skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • Pinch of salt and ground black pepper
  • 2 (16 oz) package of fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cups broccoli (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the fettuccine according to the package instructions.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter and sauté the garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the heavy cream and Parmesan cheese, stirring until smooth.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Shred the chicken breast into bite-sized pieces.
  6. Combine the chicken with the Alfredo sauce and cook until heated through.
  7. Serve over the cooked fettuccine and top with additional Parmesan cheese if desired.

Nutrition Information

Per serving (based on 4 servings):

  • Calories: 450
  • Protein: 30g
  • Carbohydrates: 40g
  • Fat: 20g
